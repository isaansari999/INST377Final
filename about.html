About 🐶

Hello! Thank you for taking the time to visit my website. I created this website initially out of my love for dogs. Additionally, research suggests that even looking at a picture of a dog can make you happy (here's an article about that).

I figured this website would be a fun way for people to also fetch random pictures of dogs for whatever purpose they needed. Whether it be social media posts, adoption centers needing pictures for their own websites/advertising, learning which breed is which, or even just sending pictures of dogs to friends!

My favorite dog breeds have to be:

  • Akitas
  • Shibas
  • Huskies

But of course, those are just a few. There's tons of dog breeds as you'll come to find while exploring this website, and they all are unique in their own way!

If you weren't a dog person before, I hope that visiting my website has swayed your opinion even a little bit!
